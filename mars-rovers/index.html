Exploring Mars Rover and Rover again

Meet The Rovers

Curiosity

Curiosity is exploring Mars to study its climate and geology and to assess whether it has ever supported microbial life.

  • Launch: Nov 26, 2011
  • Landing: Aug 6, 2012
  • Key Discoveries: Evidence of ancient water, organic molecules.

Perseverance

Perseverance is searching for signs of ancient life and collecting samples for future return missions.

  • Launch: Jul 30, 2020
  • Landing: Feb 18, 2021
  • Key Discoveries: Signs of ancient life, first sound recordings, oxygen production.
